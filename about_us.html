About Us

Trekka is the emerging of sports event management, customised expedition and outdoor education programs for youth and adults.
Experience Trekka on one of our sports event, backpacking trips, mountaineering trips, adventure expeditions, outdoor leadership program and many other offerings.

Trekka is an Indonesian company and fully Indonesian-owned especially to promote adventure sports tourism, expedition and outdoor leadership program.
Our approach to explore the hidden gems of nature. Trekka bring people on expedition in Indonesia with experienced guides and well-design programs. We develop and integrate experience and skill-building on development of characters.
OUR VISION

Connect people with the nature. Promote healthy and positive life style. Serve people and the environment.

OUR MISSION

Organize sports event, expedition and outdoor activities. Provide the breathtaking adventure spot journey. Provide the most memorable travel experience to clients. Provide the integrate outdoor leadership program.
